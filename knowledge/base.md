Our core product is an all-in-one platform for managing customer relationships, scheduling, and data collection. It includes:

We integrate with popular tools including Slack, Google Calendar, Zapier, and more. Our REST API allows you to build custom integrations.

## FAQ

### How do I install the chat widget?
Add our JavaScript snippet to your website, just before the closing `</body>` tag. You can find your unique snippet in the dashboard under Settings > Widget.

### Can I use my own domain?
Yes! Enterprise plans include custom domain support for the widget and booking pages.

### Do you offer a free trial?
Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

### What's your uptime guarantee?
We guarantee 99.9% uptime for Pro and Enterprise plans.

### How do I contact support?
- Free plan: Community forum
- Pro plan: Email support (support@acme.com)
- Enterprise: Dedicated Slack channel + phone support

### Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export all your conversations, contacts, and form responses as CSV at any time.
